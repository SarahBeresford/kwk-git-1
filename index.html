Hello! My name is Sarah Beresford and I am a freshman at CUNY Bronx Community College, majoring in computer science. I am a member of my school's biology club and hoping to pursue a career in bioinformatics, but when it comes to computer science, I know the possibilities are endless; so, I am keeping an open mind and continuing to discover how computer science can intersect with my other interests.

I am a proud 3x Kode With Klossy scholar (and a 1x Code-A-Bration participant), forever grateful that Serena Williams promoted the program on her Instagram page way back in 2019! Because of it, I have grown to love coding, data analysis, assistive AI, and I am eager to pick up as many computer science skills as I possibly can——for fun and for my career.

When I'm not studying, I love to watch women's sports (basketball, tennis, gymnastics, track), films and TV, read books (I'm big on philosophy right now. cr: Thus Spoke Zarathustra by Friedrich Nietzsche), listen to music, go on walks and runs, and watch history and geography videos on YouTube.
